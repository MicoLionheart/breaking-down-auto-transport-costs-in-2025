Key Factors Influencing Auto Transport Costs

1. Distance

The distance between the pickup and delivery locations is one of the most significant factors affecting auto transport costs. Longer distances typically result in higher costs, but AmeriFreight ensures competitive rates for cross-country shipping.

2. Vehicle Type

The size, weight, and type of vehicle being shipped can impact the cost. Larger or heavier vehicles, such as SUVs or trucks, may incur higher fees compared to smaller cars.

3. Transport Type

Customers can choose between open auto transport and enclosed auto transport. While open transport is more cost-effective, enclosed transport offers additional protection for luxury or classic cars at a higher price.

4. Seasonal Demand

Auto transport costs can fluctuate based on seasonal demand. For example, summer months often see higher prices due to increased demand, while winter months may offer more affordable rates.

5. Expedited Shipping

For customers needing faster delivery, expedited shipping is available at an additional cost. This service ensures timely delivery for urgent needs.
